About AMCAG

Leading professional development and governance for medicine counter assistants nationwide

🎯

Our Mission

To advance the professional competence of medicine counter assistants, promote ethical standards, and ensure accessible quality pharmaceutical services throughout Ghana through continuous education, advocacy, and unified governance.

👁️

Our Vision

To be the leading professional body recognized for excellence in pharmaceutical care, setting the standard for medicine counter assistant practice across West Africa and beyond.

Our Story

AMCAG History

The Association of Medicine Counter Assistants of Ghana (AMCAG) was established to address the growing need for professional organization and standardization among medicine counter assistants serving communities across Ghana.

Since our founding, we have grown from a small group of dedicated professionals into a nationwide organization representing over 15,000 members across all 16 regions of Ghana.

Today, AMCAG stands as a pillar of professional excellence, working closely with the Ministry of Health, regulatory bodies, and international partners to ensure the highest standards of pharmaceutical care delivery.
