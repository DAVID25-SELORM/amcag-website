Who We Are

About AMCAG

The Association of Medicine Counter Assistants of Ghana (AMCAG) is the premier professional body representing medicine counter assistants across the nation.

We are committed to professional development, ethical practice, and ensuring quality pharmaceutical care reaches every Ghanaian community.

Learn More About Us

Our Mission

To advance the professional competence of medicine counter assistants, promote ethical standards, and ensure accessible quality pharmaceutical services throughout Ghana.
